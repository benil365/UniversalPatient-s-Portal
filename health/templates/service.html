Global Health Limited

Our Services

Electronic Health Records (EHR) System

We offer a comprehensive electronic health records system that allows healthcare organizations to create, store, and manage patient health records securely and efficiently. Our EHR system provides easy access to patient information, simplifying workflows and enhancing patient care.

Interoperability Solutions

We facilitate seamless data exchange between healthcare providers and systems through our interoperability solutions. Our platform enables secure and standardized communication, allowing for the exchange of patient records and ensuring continuity of care.

Data Analytics and Reporting

We offer advanced data analytics and reporting capabilities to extract valuable insights from health records. Our platform helps healthcare organizations analyze trends, identify patterns, and generate meaningful reports, enabling data-driven decision-making and improving overall operational efficiency.

Patient Portal

Our patient portal empowers individuals to access and manage their health records securely. Patients can view their medical history, test results, and appointment schedules, communicate with healthcare providers, and actively participate in their own care.

Patient Doctor linking

Using our online platform our doctors and physicians are able to link with patients for consultation.

Mobile Applications

We develop intuitive and user-friendly mobile applications that provide convenient access to health records on the go. Our mobile apps enable healthcare professionals to review patient information, update records, and stay connected with their patients anytime, anywhere.
